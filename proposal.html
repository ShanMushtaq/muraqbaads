01 — About MURAQBA

Who We Are

MURAQBA (مراقبہ) is a results-first digital marketing agency based in Kasur / Lahore, Pakistan. Founded by Shan Mushtaq — a certified Full Stack Digital Marketer trained at IDEOVERSITY, Lahore — we specialise in Facebook and Instagram advertising for clothing brands and digital product sellers.

The agency was born from a real problem: when the founder's sister launched her clothing brand, there was no system behind it. No strategy, no content, no lead generation. MURAQBA was built to solve exactly that — for thousands of brands facing the same challenge.

03 — Project Scope

What's Covered

What MURAQBA Delivers

  • Facebook & Instagram ad campaign creation and management
  • Content creation (posts, captions, stories, calendar)
  • Full brand setup from scratch (pages, bio, highlights, design)
  • Lead generation campaigns with conversion tracking
  • 7-day action plan and 30-day growth roadmap
  • Monthly performance reporting and next-month strategy
  • WhatsApp communication and updates every 3–4 days
  • Competitor analysis and audience profiling

Outside Our Scope

  • Full-scale website development
  • Product photography or video production shoots
  • Guaranteed viral content (no legitimate agency can)
  • Campaigns with less than PKR 5,000/month ad budget
  • Starting work without minimum 10 product images
  • Promising results in 3–5 days
  • Any service not specified in the written agreement

What You Need to Provide Before We Begin

Minimum 10 product photos or videos (good quality)
Facebook page and Instagram profile admin access
Agreed monthly ad budget (separate, minimum PKR 5,000)
Brand preferences: colors, tone, competitor references
WhatsApp number for primary communication
30% advance payment to cover setup costs
