2
Payment Terms
MURAQBA operates on a results-first model. The client pays a 30% advance upfront, and the remaining 70% is due only after the agreed milestone is achieved and demonstrated to the client.
Advance Amount (30%)
Balance Amount (70%)
  • Advance (30%): Due before work begins. Covers setup, onboarding, and initial content creation.
  • Remaining (70%): Due within 3 days of milestone being achieved and demonstrated to the client.
  • Ad Budget: The client's monthly ad spend (minimum PKR 5,000) is separate from the agency fee and is paid directly to Meta by the client.
  • Late payment: Payments delayed beyond 5 days after milestone achievement may result in work being paused until settled.
  • Currency: All fees are in Pakistani Rupees (PKR) unless otherwise agreed in writing.
3
Refund Policy

MURAQBA takes client satisfaction seriously and operates with full transparency. The following refund terms apply:

  • Within 3 days of campaign launch: If the client is unsatisfied for any reasonable cause, the 30% advance is fully refundable.
  • After 3 days: No refunds are issued. However, MURAQBA commits to continuing work until the agreed milestone is reached before collecting remaining payment.
  • No refund is issued if the client fails to provide required assets, approvals, or access within agreed timelines, directly causing delays to campaign delivery.
  • No refund is issued if the client terminates the agreement after work has begun, except within the 3-day campaign launch window above.
4
Revision Policy
  • Ad Campaigns: Ad creatives, audience targeting, bidding strategies, and all campaign management decisions are made and managed by MURAQBA professionally. Client revisions to ad campaigns are not included — this ensures results are not compromised by untested changes.
  • Content Creation: Each client receives 3 free revision rounds per month for posts, captions, and creatives.
  • Additional revisions beyond 3 rounds per month are charged at PKR 500 per revision request.
  • Revision requests must be submitted within 24 hours of content delivery. Content approved or not responded to within 24 hours will be published as-is.
  • Revision requests must be specific and actionable. General feedback such as "make it better" is not accepted without clear direction.
5
Ad Account & Asset Ownership
The client retains full ownership of their Facebook page, Instagram account, Meta Ads account, and all brand assets at all times. MURAQBA is granted temporary access only.
  • Client's accounts: All social media pages, ad accounts, and brand assets belong exclusively to the client. MURAQBA is granted temporary admin or manager access to manage campaigns on the client's behalf.
  • Access removal: The client may remove MURAQBA's access at any time. Doing so will be treated as contract termination per Clause 6.
  • Delivered content: Original content created by MURAQBA for the client becomes the client's property upon receipt of full payment.
  • MURAQBA's intellectual property: Campaign strategies, templates, frameworks, and internal processes remain the intellectual property of MURAQBA and may not be resold or distributed by the client.
  • Ad Spend Liability: Money spent on ads is managed at the client's own risk. MURAQBA holds no financial liability for ad spend outcomes beyond professional best-practice management.

Contract Duration & Termination

7
Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality over all shared business information for the duration of the agreement and for 2 years following its end. This includes:

  • Client's product information, pricing, sales data, customer data, and business strategies
  • MURAQBA's proprietary campaign strategies, ad templates, targeting frameworks, and internal processes
  • Neither party will share the other's confidential information with any third party without prior written consent
  • MURAQBA may use anonymised, aggregated results (e.g. "increased leads by 40% in 30 days") for portfolio and marketing purposes — unless the client explicitly objects to this in writing within 7 days of signing
  • Breach of confidentiality by either party may result in immediate contract termination and legal remedy
8
Client Responsibilities

The client agrees to provide all of the following before MURAQBA begins work. Failure to provide these on time may delay results without penalty to MURAQBA:

  • Minimum 10 product photos or videos of acceptable quality — clear, well-lit, and product-focused
  • Facebook page and Instagram profile admin or manager access granted to MURAQBA within 24 hours of signing
  • Agreed monthly ad budget — separate from agency fee — minimum PKR 5,000 available and ready
  • Timely responses to content approval requests within 24 hours of delivery
  • Honest and accurate information about products, pricing, inventory, and business goals
  • A working WhatsApp number for primary day-to-day communication
Delays caused by the client's failure to provide required materials, approvals, or access will not be counted against MURAQBA's milestone timeline or used as grounds for refund claims.
9
Dispute Resolution
  • Both parties agree to resolve any disputes through direct communication first — minimum 3 documented good-faith attempts via WhatsApp or email before escalation.
  • If direct resolution is unsuccessful within 14 days, both parties agree to seek a neutral mediator before pursuing any formal legal action.
  • This agreement is governed by the laws of Pakistan. Any legal proceedings, if required, will be conducted in Lahore, Punjab.
  • MURAQBA's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the client in the preceding 30-day period.
