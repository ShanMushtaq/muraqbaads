05 — Terms & Conditions

How We Work

Payment Terms

  • 30% advance required before work begins to cover setup costs
  • Remaining 70% due only after agreed milestone is achieved
  • Ad budget is separate from agency fee and paid directly by client
  • Minimum ad budget of PKR 5,000/month required for campaigns
  • Late payment (5+ days after milestone) may result in work pause

Content & Revisions

  • Content creation includes 3 free revision rounds per month
  • Revision requests within 24 hours of content delivery
  • Approved content published as-is after 24 hours
  • Additional revisions charged at PKR 500 per round
  • Ad creative decisions managed by MURAQBA professionally

Client Requirements

  • Minimum 10 product photos or videos before work begins
  • Facebook page and Instagram admin access required
  • Timely responses to content approval (within 24 hours)
  • WhatsApp number for primary communication
  • Honest information about products, pricing, and goals

Refund & Termination

  • Refund available within 3 days of campaign launch if unsatisfied
  • After 3 days: no refund but work continues to milestone
  • Either party may terminate with 7 days written notice
  • All completed work delivered to client within 48 hours of exit
  • Prices valid for 2026 — subject to review for new contracts
